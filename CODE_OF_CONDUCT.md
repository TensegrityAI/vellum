# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][covenant] as its Code of
Conduct. By participating, you are expected to uphold it.

The full text is available at:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

In short: we are committed to a harassment-free, welcoming, and respectful experience for
everyone, regardless of background or identity. Be kind, assume good faith, and help keep
this community healthy.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately at **conduct@akaisys.com**.
All reports are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and reporter confidentiality
is respected.

[covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
