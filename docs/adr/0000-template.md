# ADR-NNNN: <short, decision-shaped title>

> Architecture Decision Record. One decision per file. Keep it terse and
> grounded in the design (`docs/plans/2026-06-15-vellum-design.md`).
> Frontmatter `status` is one of: `proposed`, `accepted`, `superseded`, `deprecated`.

## Context

What situation, constraint, or force makes this decision necessary? State the
problem and what would be ambiguous or risky if the choice were left implicit.
Reference the relevant section of the design doc or plan.

## Decision

The choice, stated plainly. Name the option taken and, where useful, the options
rejected and why. If the decision leaves a deliberate seam for future change,
record where that seam lives.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result. Include the positive
outcomes, the costs accepted, and any follow-up work this decision schedules for
a later increment.
